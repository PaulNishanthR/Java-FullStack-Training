JPA(Java persistance API) concepts 
    ->Annotations
    ->@Entity
        - @Primary
        - @Id
        - @Generatevalue
        - @Table(name = "table_person")
        - @Column(name = "address")
        - @Transaction
        - @persistant
        - @Getter
        - @Setter
        - @NoArgsConstructor
        - @AllArgsConstructor
    ->Relations
        - @OneToOne
        - @OneToMany 
        - @ManyToOne 
        - @ManyToMany
        - @JoinColumn
